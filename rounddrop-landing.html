Frequently asked questions

Is RoundDrop free?

Free for founders. Investors get free basic access with premium features available for a subscription.

How is this different from AngelList or a pitch deck database?

RoundDrop creates exclusivity and urgency. Rounds drop weekly like a launch, not a static listing. Think Product Hunt for fundraising.

How are investors verified?

We verify via fund pages, LinkedIn, and investment history before granting access to rounds.

Can investors see my full deck just by browsing?

Follows show interest. Accessing your full deck and requesting an intro requires explicit action — you stay in control.

When does RoundDrop launch?

Q2 2026. The first cohort is limited to 10 founders and 50 investors.
