Investment thesis

Pre-seed and seed checks for technical founders building deeply-opinionated software in regulated industries — law, healthcare, finance. We underwrite for unusual domain insight.

What I look for in a pitch deck

1. A sharp one-liner that makes me understand the wedge in 15 seconds. 2. A slide on what's broken today — specific, with numbers. 3. Your unique distribution path. I pass on anything that looks like "we'll do paid ads." 4. Team slide: why YOU? What insight do you have no one else does? 5. Early traction. Revenue, LOIs, waitlist — whatever's the sharpest signal.

How I assess founders & companies

Two meetings, usually. First is a 30-min Zoom — I'm assessing speed of thought and domain depth. Between meetings I reference-check 3 people in your network. Second meeting is in-depth on GTM and team. Decision within 10 days of first meeting.
